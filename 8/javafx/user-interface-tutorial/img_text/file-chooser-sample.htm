This illustration shows a View Pictures window. The window is divided into two parts.

The left part is the directory structure, which contains the following items: Desktop, Downloads, Favorites, Links, My Documents, My Music, My Pictures, My Videos, Saved Games, Searches, Computer, System (C:), and Data (D:). The My Pictures item is selected.

The right part of the window contains the following images (from left to right):

  • crayfish.png that shows a dish with boiled crayfish

  • flower.jpg that shows several garden flowers in the flower bed.

  • peach.jpg that shows a peach with leaves on a glass saucer

  • squirrel.jpg that shows a small seated squirrel

Under the directory structure and the pictures are two combo boxes. The left combo box is editable and it is labeled "File name". The right combo box has the All Images(*.*) selected.

Two buttons are located under the combo boxes. From left to right they are Open and Cancel. The Open button is highlighted with a blue border.
