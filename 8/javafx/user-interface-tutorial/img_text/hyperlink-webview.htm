This illustration shows an application with a row of hyperlinks and an embedded browser.

From left to right the hyperlinks are: Products, Education, Partners, and Support. The hyperlinks are accompanied by icons. The Products icon shows an application window, the Education icon shows a person holding a book, the Partners icon depicts a group of people, and the Support icon shows an open box. The Partners hyperlink is blue; the others are black.

An embedded browser is below the hyperlinks. The browser renders the Oracle PartnerNetwork page.
