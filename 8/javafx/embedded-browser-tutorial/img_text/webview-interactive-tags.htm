This illustration shows a WebViewSample application with the loaded HTML page. The page contains two sections: Download Statistics and Hard Disk Availability.

The Download Statistics section shows a progress indicator that is blue and it shows progress of 25%. This section also provides the downloading details (from top to bottom): Size: 1,7 MB; Server: oracle.com; and Estimated time: 2 min.

The Hard Disk Availability section is located under the Download Statistics section and it contains two meter controls that are green. They indicate the availability of disk System (C:) and disk Data (D:).
